Guodong Ye, PhD, Guangdong Ocean University

Guodong Ye is currently a Professor in the Faculty of Mathematics and Computer Science, Guangdong Ocean University, China. He received the Ph.D. degree from the Department of Electronic Engineering, City University of Hong Kong, in 2015, and was a Post-Doctoral Fellow at the College of Information Science and Electronic Engineering of Zhejiang University from 2016 to 2018. His research interests include applied cryptography and image encryption. He has published over 40 refereed journal articles and conference papers in these areas.

Qiuzheng Lin, PhD, Shenzhen University

Qiuzheng Lin received the B.S. degree from Zhaoqing University, Zhaoqing, China, in 2007, the M.S. degree from Shenzhen University, Shenzhen,China, in 2010, and the Ph.D. degree from the Department of Electronic Engineering, City University of Hong Kong, Hong Kong, in 2014. He is currently an Associate Professor with the College of Computer Science and SoftwareEngineering, Shenzhen University. He has published over 10 research papers since 2008. His current research interests include artificial immune systems, multiobjective optimization, and dynamic systems.

Fei Chen, PhD, Shenzhen University

Fei Chen is an Associate Professor at College of Computer Science and Engineering, Shenzhen University, China. He received a PhD degree in computer science and engineering at The Chinese University of Hong Kong. He visited the Distributed Systems Group at Vienna University of Technology in 2014, the Database Team of the Alibaba Group in 2013, and the State Key Laboratory of Information Security of the Chinese Academy of Science in 2009. His research interests include information and network security, data protection and privacy. He serves in the technical committee of Computer Communications; he also serves in the program committee of recent conferences, e.g., IEEE Blockchain 2021, ACISP 2020, ML4CS 2020, etc.

Leo Y. Zhang, PhD, Deakin University

Dr Leo Zhang is currently a Lecturer in the School of Information Technology, Deakin University, Australia. He received the Ph.D. degree from the Department of Electronic Engineering, City University of Hong Kong, in 2016, and was a Post-Doctoral Fellow at the Department of Computer Science of City University of Hong Kong before joining Deakin. He held various research positions with the City University of Hong Kong, the University of Macau, the University of Ferrara, Ferrara, Italy, and the University of Bologna, Bologna, Italy. His current research interests include applied cryptography and AI-related security. He has published over 50 refereed journal articles and conference papers in these areas and received more than 1,400 citations according to Google Scholar. Dr. Zhang is an Associate Editor of International Journal of Bifurcation and Chaos, an academic editor of Security and Communication Networks, and has been a PC member for quite some number of academic conferences, including NSS-18/19/20, DSC-19, PRDC-20/21, EAI Qshine-20.

Junwei Zhou, PhD, Wuhan University of Technology

Junwei Zhou received the Ph.D. degree from the Department of Electronic Engineering,City University of Hong Kong, China, in 2014. He is currently an Associate Professor with the School of Computer Science and Technology,Wuhan University of Technology, China. He is the Chutian Scholar of computer science and technology of Hubei province, China, in 2015.His current research interests include computer vision, biometrics, and multimedia security. Currently, he is the associate editor of HKIE Transactions. He has servered as the PC of Coling, EMNLP.
